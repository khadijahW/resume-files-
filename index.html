About Me

I am an Information Systems Security Specialist with a diverse background. I hold a Master's degree in Cybersecurity Information Assurance, and Bachelors Degree in Mechanical Engineering. My journey into cybersecurity stemmed from a passion for safeguarding digital assets and ensuring the integrity of information systems. Currently, my role involves implementing robust security measures and conducting comprehensive risk assessments, with a focus on government environments. I perform regular scans using ACAS (Assured Compliance Assessment Solution) to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities, contributing to the security of critical infrastructure. Beyond my professional responsibilities, I am enthusiastic about exploring new technologies and engaging in both cybersecurity and cloud-related projects. Whether it's setting up secure cloud environments or developing innovative cybersecurity solutions, I enjoy applying my skills to address contemporary challenges.
